Common questions
Is my donation secure?
The Havn Project is a youth-led project. Donations are processed securely via Stripe.
Can I see where my money goes?
Yes. We're committed to radical transparency. When we spend donations, we'll publish exactly where every dollar went — right here on this site.
What's the 7% fee?
Stripe charges a standard ~2.9% + 30¢ processing fee per donation. That's it. No platform fees, no admin cuts. The rest goes entirely to The Havn Project's mission.
Can I donate supplies instead of money?
Yes — we accept school supplies, tech equipment, food, and hygiene products. Email us at havn@thealxlabs.ca to coordinate a drop-off or pickup in Toronto.
Who runs The Havn Project?
One person — Alexander, a 13-year-old developer and student in Toronto. No team, no employees. Every dollar of donation goes to the mission, not salaries.
