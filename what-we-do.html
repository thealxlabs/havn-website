Our Work

What The Havn
Project does.

Four focus areas. All starting in Toronto. All built to be honest about where we are.

Focus areas
01
Food &
Supplies

We organize supply drives and purchase food, hygiene kits, and warm clothing for homeless individuals in Toronto. Distributed directly — no middleman.

This is the most immediate thing we can do. Someone needs a meal or a warm jacket — we get it to them. No bureaucracy, no waiting list.

Planning first drive
02
School Tech
& Supplies

We donate computers, Chromebooks, and school supplies to under-resourced schools in Toronto. Many schools have aging buildings and outdated technology — students deserve better tools to learn with.

We work directly with school administrations to understand what they actually need, then raise funds and deliver. No guessing, no wasted resources.

Planning first delivery
03
Shelter
Fundraising

We raise funds and donate to vetted shelters in Toronto. Shelters have been doing this work for years — we're not here to compete with them, we're here to send them more resources.

We haven't partnered with any shelters yet. When we do, we'll list them publicly and show exactly how much we've donated to each one.

Outreach not started
04
Student
Support

Students facing financial hardship get school supplies and essentials. Being broke shouldn't mean falling behind in class or dropping out.

This includes students who are homeless or housing-insecure, as well as students from low-income families who can't afford basic supplies.

How donations work
Step 01
You donate

Donations are processed securely through Stripe directly to The Havn Project. Major cards accepted.

Step 02
Stripe processes it

Stripe deposits funds directly to The Havn Project. Every dollar spent will be published on this site.

Step 03
We spend it on the mission

Funds go to school tech, supplies, shelter donations, or student support. We publish every expense publicly.
